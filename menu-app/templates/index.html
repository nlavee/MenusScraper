Dhall Scraper

Like to eat something in Dhall? Get notifications for it.

Get Started

We Help You Know Ahead

It is extremely frustrating to get to Dhall and realize that your favorite food has been finished because it was a popular item. You can avoid it by knowing ahead of time what's being served. You can search for the current menu of the day.

We Give You Notifications

Yes that's right, you can set notifications for certain keywords (like "beef", "marinated", "chicken"), and we'll send you notification when we see that an item of interest is being served that day.
